Tiramisu

Home

Description

ou will be amazed how silky rich and yet LIGHT this Tiramisu tastes!

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • ½ cup caster sugar
  • ½ tsp vanilla extract
  • 8 oz mascarpone
  • 1¼ cups hot espresso
  • 6.5 oz lady fingers, pavesini, or savoiardi biscuits
  • Cocoa

Steps

  1. Beat yolks and sugar in stand mixer or electrc beater on speed 6-7 for 10 minutes or until it changes from yellow to pale yellow and is thick.
  2. Add vanilla and mascarpone, beat until just combined and smooth. Transfer mixture to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Clean bowl and whisk. Add egg whites and beat until stiff (3 minutes on speed 7).
  4. Add ½ the cream mixture into egg whites. Gently fold through. When most incorporated, add the remaining cream mixture and fold through until just combined — don't bash out all the air in the egg whites!
  5. Mix cofee and liquor together. Quickly dip biscuits in and line the bottom of a 8" square dish.
  6. Sprea over half the cream, then top with another layer of coffee-dipped biscuits.
  7. Spread with remaining cream.
  8. Cover and refrigerate for at least 4-5 hours, preferably overnight.
  9. Dust with cocoa powder just before serving — either before you cut or after plating.
